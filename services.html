Stump Grinding

Stump grinding.

Tree's gone but the stump is still there making your yard hard to mow. We grind stumps below grade so you can re-sod, replant, or just be done with it.

Available as part of a removal job or as a standalone service for old stumps already on your property. Most stumps are knocked out same-day.
