Note for site owner — wire up the form

The quote form looks great but currently shows a confirmation without sending. To make it actually email you the submissions, the easiest free option is Formspree:

  1. Sign up at formspree.io (free tier covers ~50 submissions/month)
  2. Create a form, copy your form ID
  3. In quote.html, change <form class="quote-form" novalidate> to <form class="quote-form" action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_ID" method="POST">
  4. In main.js, remove or comment out the form handler block at the bottom

Submissions will then be emailed to whatever address you used on Formspree. Same applies to the contact form on contact.html.
