Our Story

How Trailblazers got started.

What started as a passion for outdoor work and a frustration with the industry grew into a full-service company because customers kept asking us to come back. First it was tree work. Then they asked if we'd handle the lawn. Then they wanted a new landscape, snow plowing all winter, fill dirt for a project. We kept saying yes — and we kept showing up.

Today, Trailblazers Outdoor Solutions covers the full arc of outdoor services: from emergency tree takedowns to year-round lawn maintenance, ground-up landscape design, snow management, landscape materials delivery, and full commercial property contracts. One crew, fully insured, that you can actually reach on the phone — 24/7 for emergencies.

Local Roots

Local crew, local roots.

We're based in River Falls, WI and proudly serve homeowners and businesses on both sides of the St. Croix — Western Wisconsin (Hudson, Roberts, Prescott, Ellsworth, New Richmond) and Eastern Minnesota (Red Wing, Hastings, Cottage Grove, Stillwater). If you're not sure whether we cover your neighborhood, just give us a call — we likely do, and if we don't, we'll point you to someone trustworthy who does.

Being local means we know the soil, the trees, the seasons, and the kinds of properties around here. It also means we run into our customers at the grocery store. That keeps us honest.

A Note On Our Industry

To our friends
who are also competitors.

Outdoor services — especially tree work — is one of the most challenging and dangerous industries in the country. The people who do it well, who do it safely, who show up year after year and earn the trust of their customers, deserve real respect.

Some of the best operators in our region aren't on our crew — they're our friends running their own outfits. We've learned from them, leaned on them in tough seasons, and watched them build their businesses with the same care we put into ours.

We grew up in this industry surrounded by friends who happen to be competitors. Their excellence pushes us to be better. Our success is shared with theirs.

If we can't get to your job in time, or if your project isn't quite our specialty, we'll often point you to one of these friends — because what matters most is that you get someone trustworthy. That's how a healthy industry stays healthy. That's how our community stays well-served.
